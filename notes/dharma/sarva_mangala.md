### Adhyaya 11, Devi Mahatmyam, Markandeya Purana

## सर्वमङ्गलमाङ्गल्ये शिवे सर्वार्थसाधिके ।
##### sarvamaṅgalamāṅgalye śive sarvārthasādhike
## शरण्ये त्र्यम्बके गौरि नारायणि नमोऽस्तु ते ॥
##### śaraṇye tryambake gauri nārāyaṇi namo’stu te

> Salutations to You who is most auspicious among all the auspicious, who is benevolence herself, the one who fulfills all the objectives of her devotees.  
> The one who offers refuge, with three eyes and a bright face; salutations to you O Narayani.
