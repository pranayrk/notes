### Japa

*For different purposes, different processes can be loosely labelled as meditation. A lot of these are around fixating the mind to something in an unwavering fashion.*

**In that sense, Mental Japa can too reach states of meditation.**

1. When you chant verbally(audibly)its called **Vaikhari**.

2. Internal chanting is **madhyama**.

3. Japa with subtle perception or fixed vision of Ishta can be said as **Pashyanti**.

4. And lastly, the transcendental states are called **Paraa**.
