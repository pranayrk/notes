## Swami Sarvapriyananda on Vedanta 

[Link](https://youtu.be/JHItbV1SP_8)

* Vedanta means teachings of the Upanishads 
* The next is the Brahmasutra
* These have 555 short cryptic sayings, philosophical foundation of Vedanta, written by Vyasa
* The next is the Bhagavad Gita
* These three are the fundamental texts of Vedanta 
* Depending on the interpretation of Brahma Sutra there are different schools 
* Sankara's commentary on the Brahma Sutra became the foundation of Advaita Vedanta 
* 1000 years ago, in tamil Nadu, commented on the Brahma Sutra wrote Sri Bhasya, which became the foundation of Vishistadvaita
* 200 years later Madhvacharya, wrote a commentary which gave Dvaita 
* All talk about Brahman 
* Shuddhadvaita, Dvaitadvaita, Achintya Bheda Ved are also Vedanta schools
